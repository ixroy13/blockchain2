BLOCKCHAIN for babies

Chris Ferrie and Marco Tomamichel, created by OP

This is a ball.

You use a coin to buy the ball from Alice.

Everyone can see Alice has the coin now.

But a digital coin is invisible.

So how do we know who has the coin?

The blockchain tracks who has the coin with a puzzle!

Alice has the coin.

Bob has an apple.

Alice uses the coin to buy the apple from Bob.

This is a transaction. Now everyone knows Bob has the coin.

Bob uses the coin to buy a carrot from Charlie.

This is another transaction.

Two transactions.

They connect!

Charlie uses the coin to buy a pear from Diana.

This is another transaction.

They all form the blockchain.

Alice pretends she still has the coin.

Alice wants to buy a pear from Diana.

This is also a transaction.

But it is invalid becouse it does not fit into the blockchain.

Alice already spent her coin!

This is how the blockchain tracks the invisible coin.

Now you know BLOCKCHAIN!
